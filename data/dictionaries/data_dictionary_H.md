# Data Dictionary — Dataset H (Financial Product Baskets)

Teaching dataset for association rules / Apriori in a finance context. Each row represents a customer and each binary product column indicates whether the product is currently held.

## Columns
- `customer_id`: synthetic identifier
- `age_group`: simplified age bucket
- `income_group`: simplified income bucket
- product columns: 0/1 indicators for banking products

## Purpose
Used to illustrate product-basket analysis, cross-sell logic, support, confidence and lift.
